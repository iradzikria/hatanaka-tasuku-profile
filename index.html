Who is Hatanaka Tasuku?

Hatanaka Tasuku (畠中 祐, born August 17, 1994) is a Japanese actor, voice actor, and singer. Born to parents who were stage actors, he grew up in an environment where theater and singing were familiar. In 2006, he passed the public audition for Disney and debuted as a dubber for Edmund in The Chronicles of Narnia. Besides being a voice actor, he is also active as an artist.
